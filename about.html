About Us

Industrium LLC is a company specializing in aluminum recycling, contributing to resource conservation and environmental protection. Founded in 2024, Industrium was established with an ambitious goal—to ensure a sustainable future for future generations.

The company focuses on managing metal waste, particularly aluminum, recycling it, and returning it to industrial use. Through a circular model that eliminates additional energy consumption for primary production, the company significantly reduces atmospheric emissions. Industrium aims to create a closed-loop system where valuable resources are not lost but reused repeatedly. This initiative represents a crucial step towards sustainable development and building an environmentally responsible future.

The Aluminum Recycling Process by INDUSTRIUM

Step 1:Collecting Scrap

Aluminum waste is gathered from various sources like cans and industrial scraps in eco-friendly recycling bins.

Step 2:Sorting Scrap

Advanced conveyor systems separate aluminum from contaminants to ensure high-quality recycling.

Step 3:Crushing

The sorted aluminum is crushed into smaller pieces, making it easier to handle and transport.

Step 4:Remelting

Crushed aluminum is melted in energy-efficient furnaces, reducing the need for raw material extraction.

Step 5:Secondary Casting

The molten aluminum is poured into molds to create ingots, ready for reuse in various industries.
